Selfless Service

Throughout my primary and secondary education, I've had quotas to fill with service hours.

Middle School

In the summers between grades I volunteered at my Parish's Vacation Bible School for elementary aged children. I was a sort of intermediary between the smaller children and the adults who actually ran the show.

This period was also when I was most active in the Boy Scouts, performing service at odd regular intervals whenever the opportunity arose in my troop.

High School

Here I volunteered with SAMMinistries, an organization founded to combat homelessness in San Antonio. I also worked with Upward Transitions, a group which enables individuals with therapeutic horsemanship. This was probably my favorite.

Today

Now I am involved in the Knights of Columbus, A worldwide Catholic fraternal service organization. Much like in the Boy Scouts, I serve wherever and whenever I'm needed.
